Something Fun for Everyone

Enjoy the sounds of Stax legends as you move from the Soulsville Foundation’s Get in the Zone AutoZone Gymnasium to the Stax Music Academy, to the one-of-a-kind Stax Museum of American Soul Music.

  • Dance to live music in each venue provided by the Stax Music Academy, Stax Music Academy Alumni Band, and Stax recording artists Southern Avenue.
  • Sample Southern cuisine paired with special craft beer and snazzy cocktails.
  • Bid on items in the online silent auction.
  • Play in the Broad Avenue Rec Room, providing games for all ages.
  • Relax at the dessert, coffee and espresso bar.
  • Enjoy complimentary valet parking for all event guests (drop off at the AutoZone 'Get in the Zone' Gymnasium).
